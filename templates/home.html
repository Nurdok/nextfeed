What is nextfeed?

nexfeed is a minimalistic RSS reader. It's built in a way that lets you read your favorite subscriptions without the overhead that most readers present.
With nextfeed, you define a list of feeds you want to follow, and get a personalized URL. Every time you hit that URL, you'll get the next unread entry in your feeds.
That's it.
